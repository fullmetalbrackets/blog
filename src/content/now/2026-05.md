## This house is clean(-ish)

I found out about [Website Carbon](https://www.websitecarbon.com/), a website that estimates the CO2 footprint of other websites. Apparently [my own website emits 0.04g of CO2 per visit](https://www.websitecarbon.com/website/fullmetalbrackets-com/), which makes it cleaner than 93% of global website. I am not sure what to do with this information, but I felt the need to share it. (And pad out this post.)

## Liminal soundscapes

I like to listen to music while doing just about anything -- working, coding, cleaning, etc. More often than not I will just do a playlist or shuffle from my Plex library via Plexamp, but lately I've been preferring LoFi and ambient music for these situations. I already listen to plenty of LoFi Girl, because it seems to have the effect of making my dogs chill out and take a nap, rather than bothering me when I'm busy.

I recently discovered a channel on YouTube called [Odd Behavior](https://www.youtube.com/@Odd_Behavior) that makes custom music with a backdrop of various liminal spaces, almost like these spaces themselves inspired the sounds. It's very cool and you should check it out if you're into that sort of thing. ([Also available on Spotify.](https://open.spotify.com/artist/3noiM3XLQUv1Y4ln9vXk92?si=kQtK6IqRR7OUX1bJN_c__g&nd=1&dlsi=2df17b0cf6fd429d))
