I liked Metroid Prime 4 for the most part, but I was the target audience: The original Metroid Prime is one of my favorite games of all time, and this felt like a return to the first game's roots, but some changes were a step (or three) backwards, the story was pretty crap and the ending was just plain terrible.

I would like to think we'll see a Metroid Prime 5 some day, but I'm not so sure after this one...
