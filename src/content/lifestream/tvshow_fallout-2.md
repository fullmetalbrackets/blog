Loved the first season, but Season 2 was not as good. It feels like a lot stuff happened with little resolution, and the season ends with a cliffhanger and several loose threads.

I still enjoyed it, it did answer some questions (while posing more), and they're clearly setting up some big things for the future. Also makes me wonder how these things will affect the storyline of the Fallout 5 when it eventually gets made.

Need more Dogmeat next season, though, he was just a prop in this one!
