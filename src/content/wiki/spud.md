# Libre Sweet Potato

- 64 GB USB Drive (boot drive)
- 100 Mbps Ethernet

## Information

Bought for $35 from Amazon. Sips power and mostly acts as my backup Pi-Hole and self-hosts <a href="https://techservatory.com" target="_blank">Techservatory</a> via a Cloudflare tunnel.
